## Top Recommendations

### 1. OpenSentinel.ai - KEEP THE NAME
- **Domain:** `opensentinel.ai` (AVAILABLE)
- **Price:** ~$50-80/yr for .ai
- **Why:** You already have the brand, codebase, and docs built around "OpenSentinel". The `.ai` TLD is arguably **better** than `.com` for an AI assistant product. Companies like Perplexity.ai, Stability.ai, and Character.ai have proven .ai is a premium, credible TLD for AI products.
- **Verdict:** Lowest effort, highest impact. Register this immediately before someone else does.

### 2. MyCitadel.ai
- **Domain:** `mycitadel.ai` (AVAILABLE)
- **Why:** "Citadel" = fortress, security, protection. "My" = personal/self-hosted. ".ai" = AI. Conveys a secure, personal AI stronghold that protects your data and automates your life.
- **Vibe:** Premium, secure, self-sovereign

### 3. OpenVigil.ai
- **Domain:** `openvigil.ai` (AVAILABLE)
- **Why:** "Vigil" comes from Latin *vigilia* (watchfulness). Same guardian energy as "Sentinel" but distinct. "Open" signals open-source. Perfect for a watchful AI assistant that monitors your smart home, messages, and workflows.
- **Vibe:** Watchful guardian, open-source ethos

### 4. TheValet.ai
- **Domain:** `thevalet.ai` (AVAILABLE)
- **Why:** A valet is a personal attendant who handles everything for you - exactly what this app does. Elegant, approachable, immediately understood. People know what a valet does.
- **Vibe:** Sophisticated personal assistant, luxury feel

### 5. AegisPilot.com
- **Domain:** `aegispilot.com` (AVAILABLE)
- **Why:** Aegis = the shield of Zeus (protection). Pilot = navigator/controller. Together: "the protective navigator of your digital life." Strong, mythological weight.
- **Vibe:** Powerful, protective, commanding
